Specifications

The materials and processes behind every GMP hammer and bit — because quality in drilling tools is the difference between a completed borewell and a stuck rig.

  • EN24 / EN36 Alloy Steel

    High-tensile nickel-chromium-molybdenum alloy steel for maximum impact resistance and fatigue life. Not mild steel, not carbon steel — the same grade used by leading global manufacturers.

  • YG8 / YG11C Tungsten Carbide

    Premium-grade carbide buttons with optimal cobalt content for the balance of hardness and toughness. Significantly outperforms low-grade Chinese carbide in penetration rate and bit life.

  • Carburised & Case Hardened

    Every hammer body undergoes certified heat treatment — carburising followed by case hardening — to achieve surface hardness while maintaining a tough core. Done at certified facilities only.

  • API-Standard Threading

    All shank connections are machined to API standard thread specifications — DHD, QL, SD, and COP compatible — ensuring perfect fit with all major hammer brands and drill strings.

  • Pressure-Tested Before Dispatch

    Each hammer is pressure-tested at 110% of its rated working pressure before leaving our facility. Test date and rating are stamped on the body.
